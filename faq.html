Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and policies

Getting Started

Getting started is easy! Simply book a free consultation through our website or call us at +1 (555) 123-4567. During the consultation, we'll discuss your vision, budget, timeline, and design preferences. We'll then create a customized proposal outlining our recommended services and pricing.
For your consultation, gather inspiration photos (Pinterest boards are great!), measurements of your space if available, a rough budget range, and a list of must-haves and deal-breakers. Also, think about your lifestyle, how you use the space, and any functional requirements.
Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual consultations via video call. Virtual consultations are perfect for initial discussions, design reviews, and clients outside our immediate service area. For hands-on work like installations, we do require in-person visits.

Services & Pricing

We offer comprehensive home decor services including complete home styling, space planning, color consultation, furniture selection and placement, lighting design, wall texture and artwork, custom furniture design, bedroom styling, and kitchen & dining decor. We can handle everything from a single room refresh to a full home makeover.
Our pricing varies based on project scope, square footage, and complexity. Design consultations start at $200. Room designs typically range from $2,000-$8,000 including design services (furniture and materials separate). Full home projects start at $15,000. We provide detailed quotes after the initial consultation based on your specific needs.
Absolutely! We work with a range of budgets from accessible luxury to high-end custom projects. During consultation, we'll discuss your budget and create solutions that maximize value while achieving your design goals. We can also phase projects over time to fit your budget.
Our design fees cover the professional services (consultation, design, project management, installation coordination). Furniture, materials, and decor items are billed separately at cost plus our procurement fee (typically 20-30% which includes sourcing, quality control, coordination, and delivery management).

Design Process

Timelines vary by project scope. A single room design and installation typically takes 4-8 weeks from consultation to completion. Full home projects can take 3-6 months. Custom furniture adds 8-12 weeks to the timeline. We provide a detailed timeline in your project proposal.
Our process includes: (1) Initial consultation and space assessment, (2) Design concept development with mood boards and renderings, (3) Design presentation and revision, (4) Material and furniture selection, (5) Ordering and procurement, (6) Installation and styling, (7) Final walkthrough and adjustments.
We include one comprehensive design revision in all packages. This allows you to provide feedback and refine the design. Additional major revisions beyond this are billed at $150/hour. However, minor tweaks and adjustments during the process are typically accommodated at no extra charge.
Yes! For most projects, we provide detailed 3D renderings so you can visualize the space before making any purchases. This helps ensure you love the design and can make informed decisions about furniture and finishes.

Custom Furniture & Materials

Yes! We design and fabricate custom furniture pieces through our network of skilled artisans. This includes custom sofas, tables, cabinetry, headboards, and more. Custom pieces typically take 8-12 weeks and require a 60% deposit before fabrication begins.
We work with a curated network of high-quality suppliers, both domestic and international. This includes luxury furniture showrooms, artisan workshops, specialty fabric houses, and trade-only resources. Our relationships ensure quality, authenticity, and often better pricing than retail.
While we prefer to procure items to ensure quality, authenticity, and proper coordination, we can work with client-purchased items for a flat fee. However, we cannot guarantee the quality, timing, or warranty support for items purchased independently.

Installation & Completion

Yes! We coordinate all installation services including furniture assembly, artwork hanging, lighting installation, and final styling. Our experienced installation team ensures everything is placed perfectly according to the design plan. Installation is typically included in our full-service packages.
After installation, we conduct a final walkthrough to ensure your complete satisfaction. We include minor adjustments and styling tweaks within 30 days of completion. We also provide maintenance guidelines for your furniture and finishes. Our 90-day workmanship guarantee covers any installation issues.
We recommend being present at the start and end of installation, but you don't need to be there throughout the entire process. Our team is fully bonded and insured, and we treat every home with the utmost care and respect.

Policies & Logistics

We primarily service New York City and the surrounding tri-state area. For projects outside this area, we can discuss travel fees or virtual design services. We've successfully completed projects nationwide through a combination of virtual consultations and local contractor partnerships.

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. We carry general liability insurance and workers' compensation for our team. All contractors and installers working on your project are also licensed and insured.
Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for projects over $10,000. Typically, this includes a deposit to begin, progress payments at key milestones, and final payment upon completion. We also accept major credit cards and can discuss financing options for larger projects.
