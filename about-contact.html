About LuxeHome

Creating beautiful spaces since 2004

Our Story

Founded in 2004, LuxeHome Decor & Styling began with a simple mission: to make luxury interior design accessible to homeowners who appreciate quality and style. Over the past 20+ years, we've transformed hundreds of homes across the country, creating spaces that are not just beautiful, but truly livable.

Our team of experienced designers combines artistic vision with practical expertise. We believe that great design should reflect your personality, suit your lifestyle, and stand the test of time. That's why we take the time to understand your unique needs before creating custom solutions tailored specifically for you.

From initial consultation to final installation, we're committed to excellence at every step. We work with trusted suppliers and skilled artisans to ensure the highest quality materials and craftsmanship. Our comprehensive approach means you can relax while we handle all the details—from sourcing furniture to coordinating installation.
